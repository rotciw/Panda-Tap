Introduction

Panda Tap is an incremental game based off pandas. Your goal is to collect rice, defeat monsters, and gain money to advance. To help you, there are many upgrades to unlock along the way. The upgrades are divided into income upgrades and hero upgrades.

Rice shop

Click at the large shop in the upper right to sell rice, which gives you money.

Monsters

Click the large button on the bottom right to fight the current monster. You have a timer to defeat the monster. Each monster has its own speed, which increases each time you defeat it. After defeating a monster ten times, you will gain money and increased income. These monsters get harder to defeat as you progress.

Upgrades

After collecting enough money, you will be able to upgrade. The left column have upgrades for your rice shop, while the column at the center contains upgrades for your hero.
